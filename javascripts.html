DODGE RECT

This game is a coded version of dodge ball. The goal is to dodge and hit your opponent with the rectangle that is being projected out of your character. If you are hit, the center line will move down and you'll have less space to move. If the center line moves is low enough, you lose. The start screen of this game was developed by Denno Louis and Clarence Emile. HOW TO PLAY Use arrow keys to move and press shift to blast rectangle to opponent.
