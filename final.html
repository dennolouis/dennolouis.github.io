Final Project

This is a two player shooting game. The goal is to get your oppenent's health bar down by shooting them. You will run out of bullets if you do not collect the green amo packs.
Collect health packs to increase your health bar. You can adjust the difficulty in single player mode. Use WASD to move Denno and shoot with "1" or "C."
Use arrow keys to move JC and "." to shoot.
